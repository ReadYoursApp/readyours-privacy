What data do we collect?

We collect only minimal data:

  • Texts you create inside the app
  • Words you save to your personal vocabulary list
  • Your email address (used for account login and identification)
  • Personal information from your Google account if you use it for signing in (e.g., name, email)

We do not collect your phone number, IP address, or location data.

How do we collect your data?

We collect data when you:

  • Generate and save texts in the app
  • Tap to save vocabulary words

All data collection is limited to your interaction within the app. No data is collected in the background or without your action.

How will we use your data?

We use your data only to:

  • Store and show you the texts and words you've saved
  • Generate personalized content using a third-party AI service provider

We do not use your data for marketing, advertising, or profiling.

Do we share your data?

Yes, but only in these specific technical cases:

  • The prompt text you enter when generating content is sent to a third-party AI service provider to generate content on demand
  • If you purchase a subscription, certain purchase-related data (such as product ID, transaction time, and a user identifier) is securely processed through our subscription provider

None of this data is sold or shared for advertising purposes.

How do we store your data?

We store your data securely and responsibly:

  • All data is stored using Supabase infrastructure
  • Your data is linked to your user account (if one exists)
  • If you delete your account, all associated data is permanently erased

Your data protection rights

You have the right to:

  • Request a copy of your saved data
  • Delete your data from our systems
  • Contact us for any privacy concern

To do so, email us at: readyoursapp@gmail.com

Legal basis for data processing

We process your personal data based on your consent where applicable, or when necessary to deliver the services offered in the app, such as generating content or managing subscriptions.

We comply with applicable data protection laws, including the General Data Protection Regulation (GDPR) and other local regulations, where required.

Changes to our privacy policy

We regularly review this policy. Updates will be posted in the app.
